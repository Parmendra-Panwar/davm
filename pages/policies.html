School Policies

Warning: Failure to adhere to these rules will result in strict action being taken against the violators. We expect all students to follow the school's code of conduct diligently.

1. Code of Conduct

All students are expected to adhere to a high standard of behavior. Respect towards teachers, staff, and fellow students is mandatory at all times.

2. Attendance Policy

Regular attendance is crucial for academic success. Students must attend all classes unless there are valid reasons, and parents must notify the school of any absences.

3. Dress Code

Students are required to wear the school uniform during school hours. The uniform promotes a sense of unity and pride in our school community.

4. Bullying Policy

Bullying in any form is strictly prohibited. The school promotes a safe and inclusive environment, and any instances of bullying will be addressed promptly.

5. Homework Policy

Homework is an essential part of the learning process. Students are expected to complete assignments on time, and parents are encouraged to support their children in this process.

6. Safety and Security Policy

The safety and security of our students is a top priority. All students must comply with safety regulations, including evacuation procedures and emergency drills.

7. Technology Use Policy

Students are expected to use school technology responsibly. Any misuse of devices or software will result in disciplinary action, including potential loss of technology privileges.

8. Respect and Diversity Policy

Our school promotes an inclusive environment that respects diversity. Discrimination or harassment of any kind is not tolerated and will be addressed immediately.

9. Parent Involvement Policy

We encourage parents to be actively involved in their children's education. Regular communication between the school and home is essential for student success.

10. Substance Abuse Policy

The use of drugs and alcohol is strictly prohibited on school premises. Students found in violation of this policy will face severe disciplinary actions.

11. Field Trip Policy

All students participating in field trips must have parental permission. Students are expected to adhere to school behavior standards during these events.

12. Grievance Policy

Students and parents are encouraged to voice concerns regarding school policies or issues. A clear grievance procedure is in place to address these matters effectively.

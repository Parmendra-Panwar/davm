Frequently Asked Questions

1. What are the school hours? +

The school hours are from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM, Monday to Friday.

2. How do I apply for admission? +

You can apply for admission through our website or by visiting the school office.

3. What is the dress code policy? +

Students are required to wear the school uniform during school hours.

4. Are there extracurricular activities available? +

Yes, we offer various extracurricular activities, including sports, music, and arts.

5. What is the school’s policy on bullying? +

Bullying is not tolerated in our school, and we have strict policies in place to address it.

6. How can I contact the school administration? +

You can contact the school administration via email or phone, details are on our website.

7. What is the homework policy? +

Students are assigned homework regularly and are expected to complete it on time.

8. Are there any field trips? +

Yes, we organize field trips for educational purposes throughout the year.

9. Is transportation provided? +

Transportation services are available for students living within a certain distance from the school.

10. What should I do if my child is sick? +

If your child is sick, please inform the school and ensure they rest and recover.

11. How are parents involved in the school? +

Parents are encouraged to participate in school events and meetings to support their children’s education.

12. What are the school’s discipline policies? +

We have a strict code of conduct to ensure a safe and respectful environment for all students.

13. How can I pay school fees? +

School fees can be paid online through our website or at the school office.

14. What is the school's grading system? +

We use a letter grading system to evaluate student performance throughout the year.

15. Are there any scholarships available? +

Yes, we offer scholarships based on academic performance and financial need.

16. Can parents visit the school? +

Parents are welcome to visit the school during scheduled visiting hours.

17. How are student complaints handled? +

Student complaints are taken seriously and addressed promptly by the administration.

18. What is the school’s emergency plan? +

The school has an emergency plan in place for various situations, including natural disasters.

19. How can I get involved in school activities? +

Parents and students can get involved by volunteering for events and joining committees.

20. What resources are available for students with special needs? +

We offer a variety of resources and support for students with special needs to ensure their success.
